# Welcome to our Study

Thank you for your interest in participating in this research study.

In this study, you will explore several interactive visualizations of synthetic and real-world datasets. You will be asked to complete a small number of tasks based on what you observe in each visualization, such as identifying patterns, locating specific elements, or describing structures in the data.

## What to Expect

- The study will take approximately **20–30 minutes** to complete.
- You will interact with different visualization techniques.
- You will answer multiple-choice and short free-text questions.
- There are no right or wrong answers for many of the questions; please answer based on your own judgment.

## Instructions

- Please complete the study in one sitting.
- Use a **desktop or laptop computer** (mobile devices are not supported).
- Do not refresh the page or use the browser’s back button during the study.
- If something is unclear, make your best guess and continue.

## Data and Privacy

Your responses will be collected anonymously and used only for research purposes. No personally identifying information will be collected, and all data will be analyzed in aggregate.

## Voluntary Participation

Your participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop participating at any time without penalty by closing your browser window.

When you are ready, please continue to the next page to review the consent information.

Thank you for your time and contribution to this research.
